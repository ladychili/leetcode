# 610.\(Easy\) Triangle Judgement

```text
triangle
| x  | y  | z  |
|----|----|----|
| 13 | 15 | 30 |
| 10 | 20 | 15 |

output
| x  | y  | z  | triangle |
|----|----|----|----------|
| 13 | 15 | 30 | No       |
| 10 | 20 | 15 | Yes      |
```

两边之和大于第三边

```sql
SELECT
    *
    , (CASE 
       WHEN (x<y+z AND y<x+z AND z<x+y) THEN 'Yes'
       ELSE 'No'
       END) triangle
FROM
    triangle
```
