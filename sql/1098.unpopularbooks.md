# 1098.\(Medium\) Unpopular Books

选出「去年」销量小于10本的书，排除近「一个月内」新上的书。

```text
Books table:
+---------+--------------------+----------------+
| book_id | name               | available_from |
+---------+--------------------+----------------+
| 1       | "Kalila And Demna" | 2010-01-01     |
| 2       | "28 Letters"       | 2012-05-12     |
| 3       | "The Hobbit"       | 2019-06-10     |
| 4       | "13 Reasons Why"   | 2019-06-01     |
| 5       | "The Hunger Games" | 2008-09-21     |
+---------+--------------------+----------------+

Orders table:
+----------+---------+----------+---------------+
| order_id | book_id | quantity | dispatch_date |
+----------+---------+----------+---------------+
| 1        | 1       | 2        | 2018-07-26    |
| 2        | 1       | 1        | 2018-11-05    |
| 3        | 3       | 8        | 2019-06-11    |
| 4        | 4       | 6        | 2019-06-05    |
| 5        | 4       | 5        | 2019-06-20    |
| 6        | 5       | 9        | 2009-02-02    |
| 7        | 5       | 8        | 2010-04-13    |
+----------+---------+----------+---------------+

Result table:
+-----------+--------------------+
| book_id   | name               |
+-----------+--------------------+
| 1         | "Kalila And Demna" |
| 2         | "28 Letters"       |
| 5         | "The Hunger Games" |
+-----------+--------------------+
```

```sql
SELECT 
    b.book_id, b.name
FROM 
    Books b LEFT JOIN (                            // subquery计算去年销量
        SELECT book_id, SUM(quantity) nsold
        FROM Orders
        WHERE dispatch_date BETWEEN '2018-06-23' AND '2019-06-23'
        GROUP BY book_id
    ) o
    ON b.book_id = o.book_id
WHERE
    (o.nsold < 10 OR o.nsold IS NULL) AND           //销量小于10或无销量的
    DATEDIFF('2019-06-23', b.available_from) > 30   //非一个月以内的新书
```
