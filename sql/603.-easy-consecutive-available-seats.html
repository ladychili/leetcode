603.(Easy) Consecutive Available Seats

603.(Easy) Consecutive Available Seats

cinema
| seat_id | free |
|---------|------|
| 1       | 1    |
| 2       | 0    |
| 3       | 1    |
| 4       | 1    |
| 5       | 1    |

output
| seat_id |
|---------|
| 3       |
| 4       |
| 5       |

SELECT DISTINCT a.seat_id              -- 4前后座都空 会被选中2次 需去重
FROM
    cinema a JOIN cinema b ON
    ABS(a.seat_id - b.seat_id) = 1 AND -- 前座和后座
    a.free = 1 AND 
    b.free = 1
ORDER BY a.seat_id
