Output the name of those who didn't have sales to the company "RED"

```sql
SELECT name
FROM salesperson
WHERE sales_id NOT IN (SELECT o.sales_id  --those who have sales to "RED"
                       FROM orders o LEFT JOIN company c ON
                            o.com_id = c.com_id
                       WHERE c.name = 'RED'
                       GROUP BY o.sales_id)
```
