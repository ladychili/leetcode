# 1069.\(Easy\) Product Sales Analysis II

```text
Sales table:
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+
| sale_id | product_id | year | quantity | price |
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+ 
| 1       | 100        | 2008 | 10       | 5000  |
| 2       | 100        | 2009 | 12       | 5000  |
| 7       | 200        | 2011 | 15       | 9000  |
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+

Product table:
+------------+--------------+
| product_id | product_name |
+------------+--------------+
| 100        | Nokia        |
| 200        | Apple        |
| 300        | Samsung      |
+------------+--------------+

Result table:
+--------------+----------------+
| product_id   | total_quantity |
+--------------+----------------+
| 100          | 22             |
| 200          | 15             |
+--------------+----------------+
```

```sql
SELECT product_id, SUM(quantity) total_quantity
FROM Sales
GROUP BY product_id
```
